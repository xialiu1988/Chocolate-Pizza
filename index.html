For thie fig-swirl: Melt butter over mdeium heat in a sausepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs andtoss in the sausepace with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturerr instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Contie to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon,juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans,roughly
    chopped
